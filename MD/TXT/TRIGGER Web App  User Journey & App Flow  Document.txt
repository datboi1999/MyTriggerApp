# TRIGGER Web App — User Journey & App Flow Document

## Product Overview

**TRIGGER** is a high-performance AI-powered growth automation platform designed for influencers and musicians. The platform’s primary objective is to guide users from onboarding into an automated “Growth Engine” that activates engagement campaigns using **Anti-Fluctuation Logic** — a stabilization system that prevents unnatural spikes and maintains sustainable growth patterns.

The platform is optimized for:

* Social media influencers
* Independent musicians/artists
* Music labels and creator agencies
* Digital marketers managing creator growth

# Step 4 — Forgot Password Security Loop

User inputs email address.

System sends:

* 4-digit OTP
* Expiration timer (5 mins)

### 3. OTP Validation

Features:

* Auto-focus input boxes
* Auto-submit after final digit
* Resend OTP timer

Requirements enforced:

* 8+ characters
* Symbol mandatory

# 7. User Story — Nigerian Musician (Spotify Growth)

## Persona

**Name:** Tobi
**Role:** Independent Afrobeats Artist
**Goal:** Increase Spotify streams and visibility in Nigeria

---

## User Journey Narrative

Tobi discovers TRIGGER through a creator recommendation and visits the platform on mobile.

He sees a dark-themed landing page with animated growth visuals and clicks **“Start Growing.”**

He signs up using Google authentication and quickly verifies his account.

Inside the dashboard, he clicks **“Start Campaign.”**

He creates a campaign called:
**“Naija Spotify Expansion.”**

He selects:

* Category: Creator
* Platform: Spotify + Audiomack

The AI verifies his Spotify artist profile automatically and displays his current monthly listeners.

Tobi chooses a subscription plan and pays securely using Paystack in NGN.

Within seconds:

* The campaign status changes to ACTIVE
* AI activity begins
* The dashboard shows:

  * “Profile Synced”
  * “Engagement Stabilization Active”

Over the next few days, Tobi tracks:

* Stream growth
* Nigerian audience engagement
* Playlist reach

The analytics dashboard shows:

* Increased Spotify streams
* Audience growth from Lagos and Abuja
* AI-generated engagement spikes during peak listening hours

Tobi now has:

* Stable stream growth
* Better audience retention
* Increased visibility in the Nigerian music ecosystem
