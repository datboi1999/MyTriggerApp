
**Design System**:

This document establishes the visual language for TRIGGER. Ensuring a cohesive user experience across all platforms. The system is built upon the **dark, high-tech aesthetic** established in the splash and login screens.

### 1\. Core Color Palette

The color scheme is designed for **Dark Mode** to minimize eye strain while highlighting AI-driven growth metrics.

* **Primary Brand Color:** **Vibrant Blue** (\#0052FF approx.). Used for the primary logo icon, main action buttons like "Login" and "Sign Up", and the 4-digit OTP indicators, for core "Growth Engine" actions such as the "Campaign" dashboard button and selection states in the category flow.  

* **Background (Core):** **Deep Midnight Navy**. The primary canvas for all screens.  
* **Surface/Card Color:** **Dark Charcoal Grey**. Used to create depth for input fields, campaign cards, and analytics modules.  
* **Typography \- Primary:** **Pure White**. Used for high-level headings like "TRIGGER" and "Welcome Back".  
* **Typography \- Secondary:** **Light Slate/Grey**. Used for subtitles, placeholder text, and descriptive labels.  
* **Success/Growth Accent:** **Neon Green**. Specifically reserved for positive growth percentages and "Active" status indicators.

### 2\. Typography & Hierarchy

* **Headings (H1/H2):** Bold, clean **Sans-Serif** (resembling Inter or Poppins). Used for screen titles and branding.  
* **Body Text:** Regular weight Sans-Serif for high readability in dense data areas like the PRD-defined "Analytics Dashboard".  
* **Labels/Captions:** Uppercase or semi-bold small text for form labels (e.g., "EMAIL", "PASSWORD") and "AI Insight" tags.

### 3\. UI Components

* **Buttons:**  
* **Primary:** High-contrast blue with rounded corners and white text.  
* **Secondary/Outline:** Transparent with thin borders for secondary actions like "Google" login or "Edit Setup".  
* **Input Fields:**  
* Darkened containers with subtle grey borders.  
* **Left-aligned icons** (e.g., mail icon for email, lock for password).  
* **Secure toggles** for password visibility.  
* **Feedback Indicators:**  
* **Checkmarks:** Blue/White for verified account connection.  
* **Error States:** Red text and borders for "Invalid URL" formats.

### 4\. Visual Language & Imagery

* **The "Trigger" Motif:** A **white lightning bolt** within a blue square, symbolizing rapid, AI-powered growth.  
* **AI Aesthetics:** Use of **neural network/mesh backgrounds** on splash screens to communicate the "AI-Powered" nature of the platform.  
* **Iconography:** Linear, thin-weight icons for navigation (Home, Campaign, Analytics, Settings).  
* **Progress Visualization:** Use of **linear progress bars** (blue) for multi-step onboarding and platform contribution charts.

### 5\. Elevation & Depth

* **Glow Effects:** Subtle blue outer glows are applied to primary buttons and "Start Trigger" actions to give them a "powered-on" feel.  
* **Layering:** Elements like the "Secure Payment" module use a slightly lighter grey surface to sit above the primary background.
