# AI Agent Specification Document: TRIGGER Growth Engine

This document specifies the functional and logic requirements for the AI agents driving the **TRIGGER** platform, designed to accelerate digital growth for creators and influencers.

### 1. Agent Overview
The **TRIGGER Growth Engine** is a specialized AI agent designed to simulate human-like engagement behaviors to enhance algorithmic visibility on social media and music streaming platforms. Its primary goal is to provide measurable, stable growth while simplifying complex growth workflows for users.

### 2. Core AI Capabilities

#### 2.1 Engagement Automation Engine
The agent's primary function is to execute **automated engagement activities** that mimic human interaction.
*   **Behavioral Simulation:** Performs "human-like" activities such as likes, follows, and "Smart Replies" to boost reach.
*   **Tier-Based Execution:** The intensity and volume of engagement are governed by the user’s selected subscription tier (e.g., Starter, Influencer, or Business).
*   **Low Latency Triggers:** The agent is designed to initiate engagement activities with minimal delay following campaign activation.

#### 2.2 Growth Retention & Stability
To ensure long-term value, the agent incorporates logic to maintain gains.
*   **Anti-Fluctuation Logic:** A specialized retention system designed to stabilize engagement metrics and prevent sudden drops (churn) in followers or streams.
*   **Organic Growth Support:** The agent focuses on triggers that encourage secondary organic reactions and followers.

#### 2.3 Smart Targeting & Optimization
The agent continuously optimizes its activities based on environmental and platform data.
*   **Geographic Optimization:** Capable of "Smart Targeting," such as optimizing reach within specific regions (e.g., the Lagos region).
*   **Detection Mitigation:** Ongoing refinement of engagement patterns to remain compliant with platform policies and mitigate bot detection protocols.

### 3. Intelligence & Analytics Logic

#### 3.1 AI Activity Feed
The agent provides real-time transparency through a dedicated **AI Activity Feed** on the dashboard.
*   **Real-time Monitoring:** Reports specific actions such as "Engagement Spike" (e.g., 450+ organic reactions triggered) and "Profile Synced".
*   **Status Updates:** Informs the user when the engine is "ready to amplify" based on platform compatibility.

#### 3.2 Predictive Insights
The agent analyzes historical data to provide actionable strategy recommendations.
*   **Optimal Posting Time:** Detects specific windows for maximum reach (e.g., "Posts between 6 PM - 8 PM WAT are seeing 2.4x more reach").
*   **Surge Detection:** Notifies users of viral growth surges as they are detected.
*   **Platform Contribution:** Calculates the percentage of growth contributed by various platforms (e.g., Instagram vs. TikTok).

### 4. Technical Constraints & Future Logic

#### 4.1 Operational Requirements
*   **Scalability:** Logic must support high-volume, concurrent engagement triggers.
*   **Reliability:** The engine targets a 99.5% uptime to ensure constant growth activity.
*   **Integration:** Features auto-detection and verification for profile URLs across 12+ compatible platforms.

#### 4.2 Future Enhancements
*   **Demographic Granularity:** Future versions of the agent will support specific **demographic and gender selection** for targeted engagement.
*   **Automated Scheduling:** Transitioning from manual triggers to full AI-driven campaign scheduling and team account management.
