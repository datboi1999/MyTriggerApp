# TRIGGER Verification and Security Check Prompts

This document contains a compilation of the specific verification, validation, and security-related text prompts used throughout the TRIGGER platform, as found in the source materials. These prompts are designed to ensure account integrity, secure data transmission, and accurate platform integration.

### 1. Account Onboarding & Authentication
**Sign Up Screen**:
*   **Main Prompt:** "Join the next generation of data-driven performance."
*   **Legal Consent:** "By signing up, you agree to our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy."
*   **Security Requirement:** (Applied at account creation) "Must be at least 8 characters long and include a mix of uppercase, numbers, and symbols".

**Login Screen**:
*   **Main Prompt:** "Sign in to continue your growth."
*   **Social Auth Option:** "OR CONTINUE WITH Google."

---

### 2. Security & Password Recovery (Forgot Password Loop)
**Email Request**:
*   **Main Header:** "Forgot Password?"
*   **Instruction:** "Enter the email address linked to your account to receive a 4-digit OTP."
*   **Action Button:** "Send Code"

**OTP Verification**:
*   **Main Header:** "Verification Code"
*   **Instruction:** "Enter the 4-digit code sent to your email address to secure your account."
*   **Retry Prompt:** "Didn't receive the code? Resend Code."
*   **Security Footer:** "SECURE DATA TRANSMISSION"

**Password Reset**:
*   **Main Header:** "Reset Password"
*   **Instruction:** "Create a new password to secure your account."
*   **Specific Requirement Box:** "SECURITY REQUIREMENT: Must be at least 8 characters long and include a mix of uppercase, numbers, and symbols."
*   **Security Footer:** "TRIGGER SECURE"

---

### 3. Platform Connection & URL Verification
**Connection Status**:
*   **General Instruction:** "Link your professional profiles so our AI can analyze your current reach and initiate growth."
*   **Success Feedback:** "Successfully connected and verified."
*   **Validation Error (Manual Entry):** "Invalid URL format. Please check the spelling."
*   **Auto-Detection Prompt:** "Auto-detected" (Used when the system identifies a link automatically).
*   **Security Footer:** "SECURE INTEGRATION • DATA ENCRYPTED".

### 4. Payment Security & Financial Compliance
**Payment Authorization**:
*   **Security Disclaimer:** "Secure Payment Gateway: Your data is encrypted using AES-256 standard."
*   **Multilingual Security Label:** "सुरक्षित PAYMENT" (Secure Payment).
*   **Auto-Fill Notice:** "Email Address (Auto-filled)" (Ensures billing consistency).
*   **Terms Agreement:** "By clicking Pay, you agree to TRIGGER's Terms of Service."

### 5. Security Settings & Notifications
**Account Settings**:
*   **Menu Item:** "Security & Password" (Navigation to credential management).
*   **Security Toggles:**
    *   "Campaign Updates: Notify me on growth milestones."
    *   "AI Insights: Personalized strategy recommendations."
